OECD Digital Education Outlook 2026: Effective Uses of Generative AI in Education
This flagship OECD report synthesises international evidence and finds that general-purpose GenAI tools can boost task performance while producing no sustained learning gains; the advantage "disappears — and sometimes reverses — in exams when access is removed." Education-specific GenAI tools designed with pedagogical intent show sustained improvement. The report also finds that co-designing tools with teachers amplifies outcomes beyond what either teachers or AI achieve alone.
